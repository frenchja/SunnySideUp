Jason A. French

I'm a Data Science Fellow at Insight and the mind behind Sunny Side Up, a web app designed to lower the barrier to learning about the economics of solar energy for your particular home. I co-develop SAPA Project, an online personality collaboratory that collects information from more than 40,000 people/year. I am also the lead developer of SAPA Tools, a software package for R that facilitates analysis and scoring of large personality datasets.

My background is in individual differences and psychometrics. I studied Cognitive and Personality Psychology at Northwestern University, and am especially passionate about how data and statistics can be used to make meaningful contributions.
